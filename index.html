learning over education

"Education is something that is done to you. Learning is something you do for yourself." - Joi Ito

Learning Over Education is a new MIT Media Lab initiative that promotes creative learning: projects, peers, passion, and play. It ties together learning related research that takes place across different research groups, develops new technologies for creative learning, and uses the Media Lab as platform for new conversations about learning.
